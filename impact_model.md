# Impact Model: ET AI Money Mentor

**Total Addressable Market (TAM) based on retail data:**
- 14 crore distinct demat accounts in India (Source: NSDL/CDSL 2024 reports).
- Assuming a conservative 30% are active mutual fund investors = **4.2 crore target users**.

**Value Unlocked Per User Pipeline:**
1. **Expense Drag Reduction**:
   Switching away from high-fee Regular plans and expensive active funds into Direct/Index funds saves on average 1% of the corpus per year. For an ₹8L corpus, this is **₹8,000/year** kept out of institutional pockets and compounded directly by the user.
2. **XIRR Improvement**:
   Via rebalancing overlapping categories and mitigating systematic risk, the investor gains an extra 0.5% - 1% annualized return. Estimated value on average portfolio: **₹4,000/year**.

**Total Per User Annual Savings**: ₹12,000/year

## Societal Scale Impact
**4.2 Cr users** × **₹12,000 saved/yr** = **₹50,400 Crores per year** injected back into the wealth of the Indian middle class.

This allows families to hit their FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) goals 3–5 years faster, redirecting capital toward child education and housing rather than asset management overheads.
